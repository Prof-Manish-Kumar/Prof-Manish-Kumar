First Symposium on Pure and Applied Mathematics (2013)

Dr. Manish Kumar had organized a Symposium entitled "First Symposium on Pure and Applied Mathematics (2013)" in Greece, which is a part of the 11th International Conference of Numerical Analysis and Pure and Applied Mathematics ( ICNAAM 2013 ) during September 21-27, 2013 Rodos Place Hotel, Rhodes, Greece.

In addition to this, he had also selected the papers after peer-review, which were published in the world-renowned AIP (American Institute of Physics) Conference Proceedings.

Pure and Applied Mathematics Symposium focuses on all areas of mathematical sciences related to innovative theoretical development, methods, technologies, computer science applications, and so forth. The aim is to amalgamate research in relevant interdisciplinary areas and provide a common link among mathematical, physical, computational, life scientists, and engineers.
